# Sraventix UI Design System
## Version 1.0

> **Design Philosophy**
>
> Sraventix is not a course-selling platform.
> It is a Technology & Workforce Development Company that empowers students, colleges, professionals, and enterprises through industry-driven education and technology solutions.
>
> The interface must communicate:
>
> - Trust
> - Innovation
> - Professionalism
> - Simplicity
> - Premium Quality
> - Enterprise Readiness

---

# Design Principles

## 1. White Space First

The website should never feel crowded.

Everything should breathe.

Spacing creates premium perception more than animations.

---

## 2. Simplicity over Decoration

Avoid unnecessary gradients.

Avoid flashy cards.

Avoid glowing effects.

Avoid colorful backgrounds.

Professional companies don't compete with the content.

They let the content shine.

---

## 3. Build Trust

Every section should make visitors think

> "These people know what they're doing."

Instead of

> "This looks like another startup."

---

## 4. Consistency

Every page must follow the same

- spacing
- typography
- colors
- border radius
- animations

No exceptions.

# Overall Feel

The website should feel like

"An enterprise technology company that delivers education."

NOT

"An online course marketplace."

Visitors should immediately perceive:

- Industry credibility
- Technical excellence
- Premium quality
- Modern engineering culture
- Enterprise trust
- Academic partnership readiness

If someone visits the homepage for only five seconds, they should think:

> "This company looks capable of training engineers and delivering technology solutions for enterprises."

That perception is the primary design objective.
